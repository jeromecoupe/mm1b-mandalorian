Favourite characters

  • Din Djarin

    Pedro Pascale

    Madalorian bounty hunter with a big heart. Loves Beskar armor and has a tendency to adopt 50 years old children.

  • Grogu

    Puppets and CGI

    Hungry baby from an unknown species. His partial training at the Jedi temple gives him a relative mastery of the Force.

  • Bo-Katan Kryze

    Katee Sackhoff

    Mandalorian warrior and former ruler of Mandalore. Leads the Madalorian group called the Night Owls.

  • The Armorer

    Emily Swallow

    Leader of a tribe of Mandalorians on Nevarro. Has a knack for forging, repearing and upgrading Madalorian Armor.

  • Cara Dune

    Gina Carano

    Former Rebel Shock Trooper from Alderaan who became a mercenary. Skilled warrior and tactician recruited by Din Djarin.

  • Peli Motto

    Amy Sedaris

    Mechanic managing a spaceport on Tatooine. Manages to repair almost any ship and doubles as a great babysitter.

  • Moff Gideon

    Giancarlo Esposito

    Commander of an Imperial remnant. Child abducter, fan of blood experiments, has a few Dark Troopers up his sleeve.

  • Greef Karga

    Carl Weathers

    Leader of the bounty hunters' Guild. Gives assignments to bounty hunters and makes sure everybody follow the rules.
